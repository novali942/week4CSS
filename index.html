The begining of The Smiths

One of my favorite bands are The Smiths. They formed in 1982 Manchester, England. Their band consisted of lead singer Morrissey, guitarist Johnny Mar, bassist Andy Rouke, and drummer Mike Joyce.

What Made Them Popular?

The Smiths gained rapid success for Morrissey's poetic lyrics about longing and loneliness, along with Johnny Mar's unique guitar playing. A few songs made them popular but one of my favorite songs on this list is called Hand in Glove.

What is Hand in Glove About?

Hand in Glove is about an intimate and intense romantic bond between two men and ignoring societal judgment. Many people have said that the relationship between the two men is purley friendship, but I don't believe that. The reason why it's my favorite song is because I love the beat, Morrissey's really strong and emotional voice, and of course the lyrics.
